MAIN HEADING: Innovation

SUBHEADING: Building Sectoral Growth through Innovation and Incubation

---

PRIMARY DESCRIPTION:
JECP's Centres of Excellence (CoE) function as hubs designed to drive sector-based growth in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities of India, with initial concentration on Eastern Uttar Pradesh. Each CoE facilitates innovation, incubation, and enterprise development while fostering sustainable growth through collaboration, research, and implementation.

---

VISION STATEMENT:
"To drive incubation - and innovation - led sectoral growth through Middle India Enterprises, enabling Economic Transformation from the Grassroots."

---

CORE PILLARS:

1. INNOVATION
   - Innovation across Value Chains: Driving innovation through research and on-ground implementation across practices, technology, demonstrations, markets, ecosystems, and intellectual property. Metric: Number of innovations introduced.
   - Innovation Partnerships: Building partnerships with industry and academia to co-create scalable, high-impact innovations.

2. INCUBATION
   - 7M Incubation Support: Comprehensive support for sector-specific enterprises across growth stages (L0–L3):
     • L0: Up to ₹10 lakh annual revenue
     • L1: ₹10–50 lakh annual revenue
     • L2: ₹50 lakh–₹5 crore annual revenue
     • L3: Above ₹5 crore annual revenue
   - Focus on Women & Youth: Emphasizing women and youth as key beneficiaries and growth leaders.

3. DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMS
   - Private Sector & Philanthropy Collaboration: Implementing programs focused on sustainability and income enhancement. Metrics: Funds raised | Beneficiaries impacted.
   - Government Partnerships: Aligning CoE objectives with national and regional growth agendas.
